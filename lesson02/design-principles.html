Rule of Thirds

Girlboss

girlboss.com

The rule of thirds is a composition principle that suggests placing the main focus of an image off-center, generally at one-third of the composition. This creates a more dynamic and visually interesting image.

On the website girlboss.com, you can see this principle in action, as most of the image assets follow this rule. For instance, if you look at every plaque in the banner, you will notice that the images occupy one-third of the space, usually on the right side, while the text occupies the left side, with some white space left in the middle.

In addition to this composition principle, the website also uses repetition of decorative elements to create a cohesive look and feel.

Repetition

Knapsack

knapsackcreative.com

Repetition is a crucial design principle that involves repeating different elements throughout a design. This technique makes the design more cohesive and consistent, which ultimately strengthens it.

For example, Knapsack.com effectively uses repetition in its website design. They have incorporated dashed curve lines throughout the website, and the grunge effect is repeated in the background of some images and even the staff picture is framed with the same effect. Additionally, they have repeated certain elements like font and color changes in specific words on titles and other texts.

Using repeated elements and negative space results in a consistent and uncluttered website design.

White Space & Clean Design

Hanamurasaki

hana-mura.com

Hanamurasaki is a ryokan, which is a traditional Japanese guesthouse that showcases Japanese culture and art and also functions as a teahouse.

Whitespace, also known as negative space, is the area between design elements. It allows for a balanced and uncluttered appearance, emphasizing certain elements and making them stand out. By reducing clutter and unnecessary distractions, white space creates a cleaner and more visually appealing design.

Hana-mura.com utilizes white space strategically to create a website that exudes a sense of peace and calmness, thereby giving visitors the impression that they can enjoy a peaceful stay at their hotel. They further emphasize this tranquil atmosphere by incorporating stunning imagery and sounds in their videos and pictures.
