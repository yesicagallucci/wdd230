La Casa de las Empanadas

Founded in 1995, La Casa de las Empanadas has been a cherished culinary destination for locals and tourists alike. Specializing in traditional Paraguayan empanadas made with the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes, our establishment has become synonymous with quality and authenticity. From savory beef and chicken fillings to vegetarian options bursting with flavor, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Visit us today and experience the taste of tradition!

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a novice enthusiast, we welcome you to explore our green oasis and let your creativity bloom!
