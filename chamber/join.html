Choose a Membership

NP Membership

This membership level is tailored for non-profit organizations dedicated to serving the community.
Benefits:

  • Access to networking events and seminars tailored for non-profit organizations.
  • Listing in the chamber's directory specifically highlighting non-profit members.
  • Opportunity to participate in community service projects organized by the chamber.
  • Quarterly newsletter featuring updates on non-profit initiatives and success stories.
  • Recognition on the chamber's website as a valued non-profit member.
$0

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership is ideal for small businesses looking to establish their presence in the local business community.
Benefits:

  • Invitation to monthly networking events to connect with other members and potential clients.
  • Basic listing in the chamber's online directory.
  • Access to discounted rates for chamber-sponsored workshops and educational programs.
  • Member-exclusive resources and guides to help grow your business.
  • Social media shout-outs and mentions highlighting your business achievements.
$500 per year

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership offers enhanced benefits for businesses seeking additional exposure and networking opportunities.
Benefits:

  • Priority placement in the chamber's online directory for increased visibility.
  • Complimentary admission to select premium networking events and industry-specific workshops.
  • Access to exclusive member-only webinars hosted by industry experts.
  • Opportunity to feature articles or blog posts on the chamber's website to showcase expertise.
  • Recognition plaque or certificate commemorating your Silver Membership status.
$1000 per year

Gold Membership

TThe Gold Membership is designed for established businesses aiming to maximize their chamber involvement and brand recognition.
Benefits:

  • Premier listing and featured profile in the chamber's online directory, ensuring top visibility.
  • VIP access to all chamber events, including exclusive meet-and-greet sessions with guest speakers and dignitaries.
  • Complimentary registration for all workshops, seminars, and conferences organized by the chamber.
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized assistance and support with chamber-related inquiries.
  • Customized marketing opportunities, such as sponsored content in chamber communications and event programs.
$1500 per year
