Caacupe Chamber of Commerce

Site plan

Site Name

The name of the site is simple and self-explanatory "Caacupe Chamber of Commerce" I want busines owners from Caacupe that feel part of the chamber and create a unity from our location.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is that business owners in Caacupe work together to get more sales, better promotion, more education available and better job opportunities for locals. The idea is work together to empower the community and make the business grow.

Scenarios

What business opportunities are growing in the area?
How can I improve my sells?
how can I find trustworthy emplyees?

Color schema

This blue is the main color of the website, I chose blue to convey trust and security.

White is to convey transparency and honesty.

White and blue are the colors of the basilica of Caacupé, which is the most important thing for paraguayans about Caacupé and a comercial hit every december when thousands of paraguayans come to Caacupe to celebrate the virgin Mary.

I'll may add some other details in color, maybe grey or dark blue.

Typography

I'm going to use Domine for titles and menu This is a serif font that shows stability and seriousness and this business is all about confidence

For the the body of the page, all the text, I'm going to use this easy to read sans-serif font called Open Sans

Just in case I need to highight something or show it in a different manner I'll use this script font called Seaweed script
