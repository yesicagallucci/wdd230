Rental Agreement Information

Welcome to Scoots, your premier destination for scooter and vehicle rentals in Cozumel! Before embarking on your exciting island adventure, please take a moment to review our rental agreement terms:

  1. Rental Period: The rental period begins upon the customer's receipt of the rented vehicle and ends at the agreed-upon return time. Extensions may be possible upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.
  2. Reservation Confirmation: A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  3. Rental Requirements: Customers must present a valid driver's license and be at least 18 years old to rent a scooter or vehicle from Scoots. No one under the age of 12 years old is allowed to ride on the scooters.
  4. Fuel Charge: Fuel charge is not included in the rental fee.
  5. Mileage: : Enjoy unlimited mileage during your rental period.
  6. Insurance: Liability insurance is included with every rental. Damage insurance is available for purchase.
  7. Damage and Loss: Customers are responsible for any damage or loss incurred to the rented vehicle during the rental period. Charges for repair or replacement will be assessed accordingly.
  8. Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled rental start time may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please contact Scoots for more information.
  9. Delivery: Enjoy free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for rentals of 2 days or more.
  10. Payment Options: : Payment can be made via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.
  11. Return Locations: Rentals may be returned to either physical location of Scoots: Downtown Cozumel Location (Near Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry Dock) or Terminal Puerta Maya Location (Catering to Cruise Lines and Patrons).

By proceeding with your rental from Scoots, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this rental agreement. We look forward to providing you with a memorable and enjoyable experience exploring the beauty of Cozumel on one of our reliable scooters, ATVs, or Jeeps. Safe travels!
